I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at COMSATS University Islamabad (Attock Campus), with a PhD in Computer Science and a research background in trust-based security frameworks, wireless and mobile ad hoc networks, machine learning, and natural language processing.

Current Research Directions Ongoing projects and active supervision areas

  • A Hybrid Deep Learning Approach for Continuous Pakistan Sign Language Translation
    Building robust models for continuous PSL translation, combining temporal modeling, vision-based feature extraction, and sequence-to-sequence architectures for Urdu text and speech.
  • Deep Learning for Mental Health Classification using Urdu Text
    Applying NLP and deep learning techniques to automatically detect and classify mental health signals in Urdu-language content, with an emphasis on explainability and cultural context.
  • Packet Drop Attack Analysis using Machine Learning in IoTs
    Leveraging machine learning for fine-grained analysis of packet drops and malicious behavior in IoT environments, extending earlier work in MANETs to modern IoT deployments.
  • IoT-based Forest Monitoring System using Acoustic Properties
    Designing IoT systems that utilize acoustic signatures of tree-cutting to detect and monitor illegal logging activities in forests across Pakistan.

Selected Projects Research, prototypes, and applied systems

  • Automated Analysis of Car Deal Conversations using BERT-based QA Model
    Developed a system that uses BERT and question-answering techniques to automatically analyze car deal conversations, extracting key insights and decision points from unstructured dialogue.
  • Adaptive Routing Architecture for VANET (Funded by HEC)
    Designed and evaluated an adaptive routing architecture for vehicular ad hoc networks (VANETs), focusing on reliability and performance in dynamic road conditions.
  • Trust-based Security Mechanism in OLSR (NS-2)
    Implemented and simulated trust-based extensions to the OLSR routing protocol in NS-2, enhancing misbehaving node detection and secure routing.
  • Multi-level Work Queue Algorithm (NS-2 & NS-3)
    Implemented and analyzed a multi-level work queue algorithm in both NS-2 and NS-3, studying performance under diverse traffic and scheduling scenarios.
  • Multicast Communication over Hybrid IPv4‚ÄìIPv6 Networks
    Worked on deployment and research of multicast communication over hybrid IPv4/IPv6 networks, particularly in the context of QoS-enabled video streaming applications.

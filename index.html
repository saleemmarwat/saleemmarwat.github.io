I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at COMSATS University Islamabad (Attock Campus), with a PhD in Computer Science and a research background in trust-based security frameworks, wireless and mobile ad hoc networks, machine learning, and natural language processing.

My work bridges foundational research in network security and trust management with emerging areas such as deep learning for text, Urdu language technologies, and AI for environmental and earth systems applications.

Current Research Directions

Ongoing work and supervision areas
  • Hybrid Deep Learning for Continuous Pakistan Sign Language Translation
    Developing robust models for continuous PSL translation, combining temporal modeling, vision-based features, and sequence-to-sequence architectures for Urdu text and speech.
  • Mental Health Classification using Urdu Text
    Applying NLP and deep learning techniques to detect and classify mental health signals in Urdu language content, with emphasis on cultural context and interpretability.
  • Packet Drop Attack Analysis using Machine Learning in IoTs
    Extending packet-loss and attack analysis from MANETs to IoT environments using ML-based detection and characterization methods.
  • IoT-based Forest Monitoring via Acoustic Properties
    Designing IoT systems that use acoustic signatures of tree-cutting to detect and monitor illegal logging in forests across Pakistan.

Selected Projects

Research, prototypes, and applied systems
  • Automated Analysis of Car Deal Conversations using BERT-based QA Model
    Built a system using BERT and question-answering techniques to analyze car deal conversations and extract key insights from unstructured dialogue.
  • Adaptive Routing Architecture for VANET (HEC-funded)
    Designed and evaluated an adaptive routing architecture for vehicular ad hoc networks, focusing on reliability and performance under dynamic conditions.
  • Trust-based Security Mechanism in OLSR (NS-2)
    Implemented trust-based extensions to the OLSR routing protocol in NS-2, enhancing misbehaving node detection and secure routing.
  • Multi-level Work Queue Algorithm (NS-2 & NS-3)
    Implemented and analyzed a multi-level work queue algorithm in NS-2 and NS-3, studying performance across diverse traffic and scheduling scenarios.
  • Multicast Communication over Hybrid IPv4‚ÄìIPv6 Networks
    Researched multicast communication over hybrid IPv4/IPv6 networks for QoS-enabled video streaming applications.
